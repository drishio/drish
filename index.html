What is Drish?

Drish is a managed integration platform powered by Apache NiFi. Enterprise integrations, finally democratized. Deploy data pipelines without managing infrastructure, JVM tuning, or cluster operations.

Managed Apache NiFi

Apache NiFi is the most powerful open source data integration engine, used by over 4,000 companies worldwide with more than 10 million Docker pulls. NiFi provides visual data routing, transformation, and system mediation with full data provenance. Drish makes NiFi accessible to everyone by eliminating the operational burden that typically requires 40-60% of engineering time.

Bring Your NiFi Flows

Drish is fully compatible with Apache NiFi. Import your existing flow definitions, NARs, and custom processors. Everything works without modification. No translation layer, no import magic. Your flows, your extensions, your muscle memory.

# Drish

> Enterprise-grade integrations, finally democratized. Powered by Apache NiFi.

Drish is a managed integration platform powered by [Apache NiFi](https://nifi.apache.org/). Deploy data pipelines without managing infrastructure, JVM tuning, or cluster operations.

## What Drish Does

- Managed Apache NiFi hosting with self-serve deployment
- Full NiFi compatibility — import existing flows, NARs, and custom processors without modification
- 300+ processors and connectors out of the box
- Visual data routing, transformation, and system mediation with full data provenance
- Modular connector selection, canvas lock, and white-label branding

## Who It's For

- Integration consultants who want a branded platform for their clients
- Small integration shops replacing expensive partner programs
- Mid-market companies stuck between workflow tools and enterprise iPaaS
- NiFi users tired of managing clusters
- Teams graduating from workflow automation to real data pipeline capabilities
